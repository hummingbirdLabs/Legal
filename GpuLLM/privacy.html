Privacy Policy

Application: GpuLLM  ·  Effective date: June 19, 2026  ·  Controller: HummingbirdLabs

Short summary: GpuLLM processes all LLM inference and GPU monitoring data locally on your device. No personal data, chat messages, benchmark results, or any other information is ever transmitted to us or any third party. We do not have access to your data.

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for the processing of personal data in connection with this application is:

HummingbirdLabs
Country: China
Contact: HummingbirdLabs@outlook.com

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect, process, store, or transmit any personal data. GpuLLM is a fully offline desktop application. All LLM inference, GPU monitoring, model downloading, and benchmarking are performed exclusively on your local device. No data leaves your device.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Chat messages or conversation history
  • Benchmark results or evaluation data
  • GPU hardware metrics or system information
  • Uploaded or imported model files
  • Usage statistics or telemetry
  • Device identifiers
  • Name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers

3. HuggingFace and ModelScope Access

GpuLLM can connect to HuggingFace Hub and ModelScope to search and download AI model files at your explicit request. When you search for models or initiate downloads, your queries are sent directly from your device to the respective service's public API. HummingbirdLabs does not intermediate, proxy, or observe these requests. Your HuggingFace access token (if configured) is stored exclusively in your Windows Credential Manager and is never transmitted to us.

4. Microsoft Store

GpuLLM is distributed through the Microsoft Store. When you purchase or download the application through the Microsoft Store, Microsoft may collect data in accordance with their own Privacy Statement. We do not receive personal data from Microsoft beyond what is necessary to verify your license (anonymous license state only).

5. Crash and Diagnostic Logs (Local Only)

The application may write diagnostic log files to your local disk (in the application data folder) to assist in troubleshooting. These files contain inference backend chain information, error messages, and performance data. They remain on your device and are never automatically uploaded. You may delete them at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

GpuLLM does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). As we collect no data from any user, this policy applies equally to all users regardless of age.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR (EEA and UK Residents)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or UK GDPR:

  • Right of access – You may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification – You may request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure – You may request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to restriction – You may request that we restrict processing of your personal data.
  • Right to data portability – You may request a machine-readable copy of your personal data.
  • Right to object – You may object to the processing of your personal data.

Because we do not collect or process any personal data, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, delete, or transfer. As HummingbirdLabs does not collect or process any personal data from users of GpuLLM, the appointment of an EU Representative under GDPR Art. 27 is not required. Should our data practices change, we will update this policy accordingly.

To exercise any of your rights, please contact us at HummingbirdLabs@outlook.com.

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information and we do not collect personal information from users of GpuLLM. There is nothing to disclose or delete.

9. International Data Transfers

We do not transfer personal data internationally, as we do not collect or hold any personal data.

10. Data Retention
